2D TMDs 2026 @ NUS

Building on the success of previous 2D TMDs conferences in Cambridge and Hong Kong, we are delighted to host the 2D TMDs 2026 conference in Singapore. This gathering will unite top researchers from both academia and industry to share and discuss the latest developments and breakthroughs in the fundamental and applied research focusing on atomically thin TMDs. The conference offers a unique platform to engage with experts in various fields and industry leaders, as well as participate in insightful discussions with journal editors on publication strategies and scientific communication. Topics will span recent advances in the synthesis of 2D TMDs and their cutting-edge applications in electronics, photonics, catalysis, energy conversion and storage. We warmly welcome abstract submissions from students and postdoctoral researchers.

Topics

  • Wafer scale synthesis
  • Electronics - logic, memory, interconnects, neuromorphic
  • Photophysics - exciton, plasmons
  • Photonics and optoelectronics
  • Chemistry and catalysis
  • Quantum technology
  • Energy conversion and storage
  • Defect engineering
  • Imaging and characterization
  • Twistronics and beyond TMDs
  • Charge and spin transport
  • Theory and modelling
