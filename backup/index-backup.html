About The Conference

The Zero Project regionalizes its activities to important world regions outside Europe, with the goal of building bridges between the global disability inclusion community and regional innovation ecosystems.

The annual Zero Project India Conference (#ZeroConIndia24) hosted by Youth4Jobs in New Delhi is a unique regional forum to bring together members of the global Zero Project Network, Zero Project Awardees, and local/regional stakeholders, decision makers, and innovators from India and the whole of South Asia.

Venue Accessibility

  • Accessible Location: The conference venue is situated in a location easily accessible by public transportation and with ample parking for those arriving by car.
  • Entrance and Exit: The venue boasts wide, accessible entrances and exits, accommodating wheelchairs and other mobility aids.
  • Internal Access: Ramps, elevators, and clear signage guide attendees to all conference areas without obstacles.
  • Restroom Facilities: Accessible restrooms are conveniently located throughout the venue, equipped with necessary assistive features.

Conference Accessibility

  • Seating: A designated area with accessible seating is available for attendees with mobility limitations, offering clear sightlines to the stage.
  • Assistive Technologies: Upon request, the conference provides assistive listening devices, real-time captioning, or sign language interpreters to support attendees with hearing impairments.
  • Information: All printed materials will be available in accessible formats, such as large print or digital versions.
  • Staff Training: Our staff is trained to provide assistance and support to attendees with disabilities.

Catering

  • Dietary Restrictions: A variety of dietary options, including vegetarian, vegan, and allergen-free choices, will be available.
  • Accessible Dining: Food stations and seating areas are designed to accommodate individuals with mobility challenges.

Participation Policy

We cordially invite all individuals interested in attending the Zero Project India Conference 2024. To facilitate seamless participation, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Limited Capacity & Open Attendance: Due to limited capacity, priority will be given to those who have completed the registration process. Individuals who have not received a formal invitation are also welcome to participate. We encourage registration to assist with event planning and accommodate all attendees effectively.
  • Accessibility: We are committed to providing an inclusive environment. We will post a full list of accessibility features shortly. Don't hesitate to get in touch if you have any other questions: zeroconindia@youth4jobs.org.
  • Image and Video Rights: Photographs and videos captured at the Zero Project India Conference may be used for promotional and communication purposes and shared with the Zero Project, in accordance with their Privacy Policy. By attending the conference, participants consent to the use of their images.
